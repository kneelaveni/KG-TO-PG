Masters of Technology

M.Tech stands for Masters of Technology. It is a course that focuses on the advanced study of science and technology.The focus of the course is engineering and information technology. It is a two-year postgraduate programme with four semesters.

Master of Business Administration

A master of business administration (MBA) is a graduate degree that provides theoretical and practical training for business or investment management. An MBA is designed to help graduates gain a better understanding of general business management functions.

Master of Science

The Master in Science (MS) is the perfect two-year graduate study program if you have a relevant bachelor's degree in science and aspire to delve deep into the subject. The degree is a technically oriented program and is focused on developing analytical and problem-solving skills through lab work and research.

Masters of Commerce

MCom or Masters of Commerce is a two-year post-graduate level course ideal for candidates who wish to make a career in banking financial services and insurance (BFSI) as well as accounting and commerce sectors.
